I'm a machine learning developer currently interested in striking a balance between exciting novel research and the satisfaction of practical development. In the past, I've worked on computer vision and language modeling research problems at startups like [Project N](https://projectn.co), [Groundlight AI](https://www.groundlight.ai/), and [Nanotronics Imaging](https://nanotronics.co/). I'm finishing out my bachelor's degree at Harvey Mudd College, and in the fall I start work at the [Anima AI Lab](http://tensorlab.cms.caltech.edu) at Caltech as a [Schmidt Fellow in Software Engineering](http://sase.caltech.edu).  
 
I've previously been fascinated with questions around uncertainty estimation, model calibration and model robustness. Lately, I've been more excited to use existing but new technologies to build practical products that align with my personal values. I'm currently working with the University of Michigan's Taubman College of Architecture to develop a prototype system that [summarizes long environmental impact statements](github.com/dhpitt/nytautobrief) using new language modeling techniques.
